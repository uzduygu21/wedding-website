Travel and Hotel information

Turkey requires a Visa to enter into the country. It is very easy to get and should be purchased when you get your airline ticket. It can be done online at https://www.evisa.gov.tr/en/

We are advising on waiting to get the airline ticket from Istanbul to Izmir as we get a little closer. It is a short flight and tickets will remain cheap. We are looking into an option to charter a bus for everyone but will have more details in the coming months if this is a viable option.

We advise that you get your hotels ASAP as we were not able to block any large amount of rooms.
